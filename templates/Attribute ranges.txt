Attribute information:

   For more information, read [Cortez and Morais, 2007].

   
   1. DMC - DMC index from the FWI system: 1.1 to 291.3 
   2. DC - DC index from the FWI system: 7.9 to 860.6 
   3. temp - temperature in Celsius degrees: 2.2 to 33.30
   4. RH - relative humidity in %: 15.0 to 100
   5. wind - wind speed in km/h: 0.40 to 9.40 
  
   6. area - the burned area of the forest (in ha): 0.00 to 1090.84 
   (this output variable is very skewed towards 0.0, thus it may make
    sense to model with the logarithm transform).
